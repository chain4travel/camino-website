3.- Procedure

To apply for a Grant from the Grant Program, you shall comply with the Procedures and processes defined in these Terms and Conditions and our Guidelines.

Your application shall fulfil the following criteria: (i) it shall be a software-based project, which contributes to the advancement of the travel ecosystem; and (ii) you will need to submit the application and deliver Milestones Program according to the Procedure specified below.

The Grant Program consists of four parts, each of them described in more detail below:

3.1. Application

The Grant Program is open to natural and legal persons that have submitted a completed Grant Program application describing the Project via the Grant Program website located at https://camino.network/grant-program/.

To be accepted for the Grant Program, the Camino Foundation will review the application submitted by you. The Camino Foundation will aim to but is not obligated to, process each application within thirty (30) days after submission.

Accordingly, your application will be accepted or rejected by the Camino Foundation. If you will be accepted for the next step, they will be informed via email which has been provided in the application.

3.2. Initial Call

Once the application has been accepted as outlined in section 3.1. above, the Camino Foundation will set up an initial call to learn more about the applicant and its vision. There will also be a discussion on how the milestones are set and how much CAM is needed to achieve these. You agree to follow all requirements as set forth in the Grant Program and in further instructions that may be provided to you by the Camino Foundation.

3.3. Final Review

After the initial call, you will be informed either way of your grant acceptance or rejection. If accepted, you must confirm your agreed-upon milestones during the initial call through the issuance of a Milestones Program and the signature of the corresponding documents, the Guidelines and in further instructions that may be provided to you by the Camino Foundation.

As soon as the Camino Foundation approves the request, the delivery is officially accepted. The delivery is now a part of the Milestones Program delivery.

The time point of the acceptance is hereby stored on the chain.

THE GRANTING OF GRANTS AS PART OF THE GRANT PROGRAM IS WITHIN THE CAMINO FOUNDATION’S SOLE DISCRETION AND ALL DECISIONS RELATED TO THE GRANT PROJECT ARE FINAL AND NON-APPEALABLE.

3.4. Payment process

The Camino Foundation will work with you to establish a Milestones Program (as described in sections 3.2 and 3.3 above) to split up the compensation in CAM and to allow payment whenever a milestone is hit.

THE PAYMENT TO YOU OF THE GRANT IN CAM IS SUBJECT TO COMPLIANCE WITH APPLICABLE LAWS IN FORCE.
