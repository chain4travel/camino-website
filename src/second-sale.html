The Camino Token

The CAM is the native token that powers the Camino Network, playing a critical role in transactions and encouraging active participation within the consortium. Designed to meet regulatory standards, CAM is officially classified as a utility token, not a security. Its versatile utility encompasses vital functions within the Camino Network, ensuring its indispensable role in the seamless operation and sustainable growth of the ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions
How many tokens will be distributed?

75,000,000 CHF will be distributed in the second sale.

How can I buy CAM?

The process is outlined above and we will walk you through each step upon registration.

Registration: Start by registering using the contact form XX.

Wallet Wizard: Following the registration, you will receive a "Wallet Wizard" that will prompt you to enter basic information, including your name, xxx.

Camino Wallet: The subsequent step is to create a Camino Wallet. If you already have a Camino Wallet, you can skip this step.

KYC Verification: A mandatory Know Your Customer (KYC) verification via the provider Sumsub comes next.

Token Agreement: Upon successful KYC verification, the Foundation will generate a Token Agreement. This document will be sent to you via DocuSign along with a payment request.

Send Payment & Receive Token: Once payment is received, the tokens will be transferred to your wallet within 5 business days.

Please feel free to contact us at hello@camino.network if you have any queries or need assistance at any point in this process.

Is there a minimum ticket amount?

Yes, the second sale is open for all ticket sizes above 5,000 CHF.

Who can participate?

The second sale is the first sale open to the public. Anyone who follows the verification process can participate.

Do I need KYC?

Yes, you need to undergo a KYC verification done by SumSub. This takes about two minutes. Proof of verification is required, such as your ID.

After completing all steps and signing the agreement, the tokens will be transferred to your wallet within 5 business days.
