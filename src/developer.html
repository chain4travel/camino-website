Camino Messenger

Camino Messenger is a sleek, fast, and cost-effective communication platform for B2B travel distribution. Built on top of Camino Network, it streamlines peer-to-peer interactions with the help of bots, standardized message types, micropayments, and microservices, enabling personalized offers at scale.
