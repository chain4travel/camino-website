It is time to build the future of travel

In today’s travel industry, the pace of innovation is decelerated due to the industry's nature of servicing with hardware, machines, and people. The entire travel industry relies on standard protocols that are more than 40 years old and desperately need enhancement.

Camino is for everyone with an idea

Camino is a semi permissionless consortium blockchain that anyone can build on. There are no fees, and the only thing you need to get started is an idea.

Highly secure travel infrastructure

As we put security at the heart of the design of the Camino blockchain and our developing efforts, we are following a multi-tier approach to ensure that we comply with the best practices and highest standards of security. The Camino blockchain, by design, is a consortium blockchain and all validators need to go through a KYB (Know-your-business) process. Additionally, any wallet address coded in a smart contract also needs to be verified to be deployed on the Camino chain.
