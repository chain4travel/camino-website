How does it work

Camino Network is a blockchain-based ecosystem connecting travel businesses and developers to create innovative solutions for the industry. Participants can get rewards and have a say in the network's governance by becoming validators ensuring secure and transparent travel-related transactions and interactions.

Business Verification (KYB) on Camino Network

KYB is the powerful tool of Camino Network which solves two important issues in the digital travel space: authenticity and transparency. Emulating strategies of platforms like Twitter, which have introduced verification processes to combat bot manipulation, KYB serves as a Unique Value Proposition within the whole travel industry.
