International travel often comes with a maze of complex visa applications, creating stress and uncertainty for travelers worldwide. SimpleVisa has transformed this experience by seamlessly integrating visa services directly into the travel booking process. Through its partnership with Camino Network, SimpleVisa is now taking this approach to new heights with high speed of transacting, security, and transparency in visa processing thanks to blockchain.

{% image "./src/static/images/spotlight/simplevisa-content.png", "", "", "" %}

Transforming visa services through web3

SimpleVisa's platform represents a fundamental shift in how travelers can obtain visas. By integrating directly with Camino Messenger, the platform enables instant visa processing and secure payment distribution

{% image "./src/static/images/spotlight/simplevisa-content-2.png", "", "", "" %}

The are two powerful APIs at the heart of SimpleVisa's solution:

  • The Visa Validation API instantly confirms visa requirements based on the traveler's nationality and destination
  • The Visa Ordering API enables direct visa service ordering through any travel booking platform

This tech stack has already been adopted by over 400 Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) worldwide, automating and simplifying multiple manual processes:

  • improving customer experience through integrated visa services
  • providing new revenue streams through commission opportunities
  • streamlining new integration through Camino Messenger
{% image "./src/static/images/spotlight/simplevisa-content-3.png", "", "", "" %}

Roadmap

SimpleVisa's integration with the Camino Messenger is the first step towards new ways of handling travel documents, especially with upcoming digital wallets and digital identities. The process will be split into the following steps:

  1. Initial proof of concept with key distribution partners (interested partners are welcome to join the pilot - book a demo)
  2. Platform access expansion to all Camino-connected players
  3. Global scaling through Camino's decentralized network

The future of travel documentation

The implementation of web3 technology brings crucial advantages to operations with travel documents. It enables secure information sharing across multiple travel platforms with verifiable transaction records via automated processes through smart contracts.
