Established in 2009 in Greece, AVRA established a trusted DMC by providing top-level services to tour operators, travel agents, and groups. With years of expertise in authentic Greek experiences tailored to tourists from all over the world, AVRA has joined the web3 travel ecosystem of Camino Network. As a strategic milestone, the company has launched Greece's first online travel shop targeted at B2C customers and exclusively accepting cryptocurrency, offering a seamless and modern way to book a Greek getaway.

What makes AVRA's Greek crypto-OTA unique

AVRA's approach to web3 travel stands out through several distinctive features:

Exclusive cryptocurrency acceptance By focusing solely on cryptocurrency payments, AVRA streamlines the booking process while embracing the future of digital transactions.
NFT-powered travel experiences Each booking is transformed into a unique opportunity, with NFTs for verification of authentic reviews, access to exclusive rewards, personalized travel support.
Local expertise meets web3 AVRA combines deep local knowledge with web3 to provide travelers with crypto convenience and authentic Greek experiences.
Blockchain-secured bookings Every reservation is minted on Camino Network, providing transparent and secure transaction records.

The NFT booking process

To book, customers simply connect their wallet. Once the payment is completed, an NFT ticket is minted directly into their wallet, ensuring the booking is immutable, transparent, and secure. Along with the NFT ticket, customers receive personalized NFTs that unlock various benefits, such as loyalty rewards, exclusive event access, special promotions, and opportunities to contribute to sustainability initiatives.
