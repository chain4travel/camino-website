Bogowi is a Web3 travel platform built on Camino Network that transforms every booking into a verified act of conservation.

With each booking, travelers earn BOGO tokens, receive NFT travel passes, and vote on where a portion of booking revenue is invested ‚Äî funding local environmental projects in the very destinations they visit.

From beach cleanups to coral restoration, every journey contributes to the health of the planet ‚Äî transparently, traceably, and community-led.

How Camino Enables the Impact Engine

Bogowi integrates Camino Network's travel-focused blockchain to power:

  • NFT-based booking passes minted on-chain as digital collectibles
  • Tokenized reward system based on conservation impact, referrals & gamified actions
  • DAO governance for funding local projects voted on by travelers and hosts
  • Secure payment infrastructure supporting both fiat & crypto

The platform uses Camino's Web3 connectivity to handle identity, payments, messaging, and DAO voting ‚Äî tailored specifically for real-world tourism use cases.
