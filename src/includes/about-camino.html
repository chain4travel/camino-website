Who we are

The initiator and facilitator of the Camino consortium blockchain is Chain4Travel AG. The team of Chain4Travel and their advisors have accumulated hundreds of years of experience in the travel industry and have an eye on the current status and innovations in the travel and technology business. The vision is to create an ecosystem that will boost the travel industry by enabling a new way of dealing with B2B2C travel. Chain4Travel sets out to connect the new world of travel.
