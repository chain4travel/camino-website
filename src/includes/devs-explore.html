Explore your potentials with Camino

Camino is a layer one blockchain. As a fork of Avalanche, the blockchain features 3 built-in blockchains: Platform Chain (P-Chain), Exchange Chain (X-Chain), and Contract Chain (C-Chain). All 3 blockchains are validated and secured by the Camino consortium.

P-Chain

The Platform Chain is used to manage and lock stake, validator, and network coordination.

C-Chain

The Contract Chain provides full EVM compatibility for smart contracts.

X-Chain

The Exchange Chain is used for fast asset creation and management.
