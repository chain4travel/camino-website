Roadmap
Q4 2021
Chain4Travel AG Founded

Team formation and the start of development.

Q2 2022
Seed Investment

Closing 4.3M CHF seed investment.

Q2 2022
Columbus Testnet Launch

Development of the first B2B decentralized travel applications.

Q3 2022
Private Pre-Sale of Camino Token

Closing the pre-sale with 4.6M CHF from 300+ entities.

2024
CAM Public Sale

Focus on the ecosystem's growth with new use cases and increased transactions before listing.

Q4 2023
Camino Messenger PoC

Presentation of the first p2p messenger for the travel industry and its further development.

Q3 2023
Establishment of the Camino Network Foundation

The Foundation is set up to lead the future development of the decentralized network.

Q2 2023
Camino Network Mainnet Launch

Soft launch of the mainnet in several stages. First dApps deployed on Camino Network.

Q1 2024
Camino Messenger Testnet

"Berlin" phase deployment with a beta version of Camino Messenger.

Q2 2024
Camino Messenger Live

Cross-chain communication and distribution via Camino Messenger.

Q3 2024
Block Explorer 2.0

Enhanced user experience to intuitively navigate through the web3 travel ecosystem.

Q4 2024
Camino Wallet 2.0

Introduction of new features and improved UX/UI.

2024
Initial Exchange Offering (IEO)

Focus on the ecosystem's growth with new use cases and increased transactions before listing.

2024
Fireblocks Integration

Camino Network integrates Fireblocks to bring direct connectivity to its token holders.
