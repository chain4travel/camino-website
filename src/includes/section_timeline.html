Milestones
Q4 2021
Foundation of Chain4Travel AG

After experimenting with web3 technology, we determined that current protocols do not satisfy the travel industry’s needs and decided to create an L1 protocol for the travel industry.

Q2 2022
Seed Investment

Closing of ₣ 4.3 m seed Investment.

Q2 2022
Launch: Columbus Testnet

Based on an Avalanche fork, the first version of the Columbus Testnet is launched. Partners and supporters start building the first B2B decentralized travel applications.

Q3 2022
Private Pre-Sale of Camino Token

More than 300 organizations, companies and individuals participate in the private Camino Pre-Sale, taking in ₣ 5 m.

Q2 2023
Launch of the Camino Mainnet

After gathering 150+ supporters including 80+ validators, the Camino Mainnet launched in April 2023.

Q4 2023 - ACTIVE
PoC Camino Messenger

The PoC was presented to the network and was met with great interest from travel companies. Validators are now working to further improve the application.

2024
Initial Exchange Offering (IEO)

To make Camino accessible to the global travel industry and customers, CAM will be listed on leading exchanges in 2024 and onwards.

Q1 2024
Camino Messenger Testnet

"Berlin" phase update will enhance the ecosystem with a beta version of Camino Messenger, providing seamless p2p communication within the travel industry.

Q2 2024
Camino Messenger Live

Camino Messenger Alpha will enable cross-chain communication. This means wider connectivity and endless possibilities for collaboration.

Q3 2024
Block Explorer 2.0

Block Explorer 2.0 will provide an enhanced user experience. Navigating the web3 travel ecosystem will be easy and intuitive.

Q4 2024
Camino Wallet 2.0

Camino Wallet gets a major facelift and functionality boost. Wallet 2.0 will be packed with new features and a clean interface.
