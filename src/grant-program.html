Four-step process for Take-off

  • 1. Submit your application

    Please submit your application and let us discover your vision for the future of travel.

  • 2. Camino review

    The Camino Foundation will thoroughly review your application, ensuring that only the most innovative and promising projects move forward. We will be in touch within just 14 days.

  • 3. Schedule initial call

    Once your application has been reviewed, and we see a great fit, we'll set up an initial call to learn more about you and your vision. This is your chance to impress us with your project and passion.

  • 4. Get rewarded with CAM

    Once you have completed the first steps, it's time to receive your CAM Token, paid out after each milestone you achieve.
