Mission & values

We, the members of Camino Network, are building the layer one blockchain tailored specifically to the travel industry.

Our mission is to create a more efficient and connected ecosystem that benefits all participants travelers, service providers, developers, validators, organizations, and businesses.

In Camino Network we believe that transparency and trust are essential for a decentralized network, which is why all validators go through a KYC and KYB process to maintain the integrity of the network. Efficiency is crucial to the travel industry, that is why we are committed to building a more efficient ecosystem.

STARTUP STAGE: Chain4Travel prepares to launch blockchain run by travel industry
